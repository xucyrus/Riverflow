活動簡介

來自成都集團CDC的大陸饒舌歌手王以太，在2018年《中國新說唱》中取得全國前六強的好成績，後陸續參加《我是唱作人》、《我們的樂隊》等音樂節目，並於2022年《中國說唱巔峰對決》中與成員以「一席之地」之團隊名稱，拿下巔峰聯盟冠軍。 這位能唱又能饒的歌手在2019年榮獲華人歌曲音樂盛典年度最受歡迎說唱歌手獎，他的代表作也在YouTube累積驚人的觀看數，其中《目不轉睛》已累積339萬次觀看、《時間是金》累積128萬次觀看。 2024年，王以太將帶著他的第三張全新個人專輯《Love Me Later》展開巡迴演出，並於9月14日首次在台北舉辦專場演出，期待與粉絲們共同打造最炸的嘻哈舞台。

Wang Yitai, a mainland rapper from the Chengdu CDC group, achieved a top six finish in the 2018 "The Rap of China" competition. He subsequently participated in music programs such as "I Am a Singer-Songwriter" and "Our Band." In 2022, he and his team, under the name "One Seat," won the championship in the "China Rap Summit Showdown." This versatile singer and rapper won the Most Popular Rapper award at the Chinese Songs Music Festival in 2019. His notable works have garnered impressive views on YouTube, with "Eyes On Me" accumulating 3.39 million views and "Time is Gold" reaching 1.28 million views. In 2024, Wang Yitai will embark on a tour with his third new solo album "Love Me Later," and will hold his first solo concert in Taipei on September 14, promising to create the most explosive hip-hop stage with his fans.
